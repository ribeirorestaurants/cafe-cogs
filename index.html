☕ Connect to Google Sheets

Link this dashboard to your Google Sheet so every change syncs automatically. You'll need a Google Cloud OAuth Client ID.

  1. Go to Google Cloud Console → Enable the Google Sheets API.
  2. Create an OAuth 2.0 Client ID (Web Application type). Add http://localhost and your app URL to Authorized Origins.
  3. Copy your Client ID below (ends in .apps.googleusercontent.com).
  4. Open your Google Sheet, copy the Spreadsheet ID from the URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/ID/edit.
